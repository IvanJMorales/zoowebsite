RHINO

Rhinoceroses are large mammals that are identified by the horns on their snouts. They are found in Africa and Southeast Asia. Rhinos have an herbivorous diet so they generally eat leafy material. Unfortunately, Rhinos are hunted by poachers for their horns and are considered an endangered species.
