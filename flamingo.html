FLAMINGO

Flamingos are birds that are known to stand on one leg while the other is tucked beneath their bodies. Why? Nobody knows... Flamingos are omnivores, eating algae, brine shrimps, and insects. They are found in the Americas as well as Africa, Asia, and Europe
